WELCOME

The USSJ is a student-run journal publishing a wide variety of writing related to Slavic studies. Undergraduate work is collected semiannually in the form of translations, essays, travel writings, and other relevant works. UNC undergraduates with an interest in any Slavic-related disciple are encouraged to submit their own work for inclusion in the publication.
